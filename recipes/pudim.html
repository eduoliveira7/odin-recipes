Pudim

Description

He is delicious, affordable, and quick to prepare! With its soft texture and unmistakable flavor, this dessert is a true temptation for lovers of traditional sweets.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 1 can of milk (measured from the can of condensed milk)
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cup (tea) sugar

Steps

  1. First, beat the eggs well in a blender.
  2. Add the condensed milk and milk, and beat again.
  3. Melt the sugar in the pan until it turns brown , add the water and let it thicken.
  4. Place in a round shape and pour the pudding mixture on top.
  5. Bake in a medium oven for 45 minutes , with the round pan inside a larger pan filled with water .
  6. Stick a fork in to see if it is well cooked.
  7. Let cool and unmold.
